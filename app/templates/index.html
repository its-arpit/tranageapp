Our Overview

Transportation industry contributed roughly 6.3% of GDP and is majorly dominated by road sector. More than 50% of freight and 90% of passenger traffic are handled by road. Government as well private investments, rising exports, increasing interstate movement of goods and passengers, growing FMCG sector and rising disposable income are the major drivers of transportation industry in India. In order to boost the connectivity across country, 100% FDI has been allowed in roads and Highway sector via automatic route.

The Vision

TRANAGE believes that supporting the transportation industry with contemporary equipment and devices will certainly boost the accuracy, transparency and reliability of clients over transporters. Consequently, a team of skilled professionals and specialists are working hard to identify the major concerns of transportation and to find out innovative and apt solutions for them.

The Mission

We at TRANAGE, are aimed to setting up an enormous transportation network extended throughout India. We have dreamed for an easy yet secured online medium that can transport bulk material or products from North to South and East to West in pan India swiftly and hassle less.

TRANAGE is determined its solid presence in pan India and to register 1 million trucks with the platform of Tranage. The design and system is planned to assist transporters and truckers in improving their financials. Thus, Tranage dreams to anchor its name among leading trucking brands.
